# Implementation Playbook

## Operating Rhythm
| Cadence | Activity | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily | Team huddle on blockers and priorities | Department Lead | Same-day issue resolution |
| Weekly | KPI review and action-plan update | Marketing Manager / Growth Marketing Manager | Updated dashboard and task status |
| Biweekly | Sponsor update | Project Owner | Decision log and escalation list |
| Monthly | Business impact review | Finance / Analyst | KPI validation and savings summary |

## Standard Work
1. Review KPI trend before the team meeting.
2. Identify the largest gap between target and actual performance.
3. Assign one accountable owner per corrective action.
4. Track risk status weekly.
5. Document decisions and update the case study after major milestones.

## Control Plan
The process remains stable through owner accountability, weekly scorecard reviews, and leadership visibility into risks, actions, and outcomes.
