# Marketing Manager — Digital Campaign ROI Optimization

## Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world digital marketing performance improvement initiative led from the perspective of a Marketing Manager. The project focuses on improving campaign ROI, reducing wasted ad spend, increasing lead quality, improving conversion rates, and building a stronger reporting system for campaign decision-making.

The project demonstrates how a Marketing Manager would evaluate campaign performance, identify underperforming channels, align stakeholders, track marketing KPIs, optimize budget allocation, and communicate results to leadership.

## Business Problem

The organization was running digital campaigns across multiple channels, but campaign performance was inconsistent. Some campaigns generated traffic without strong conversion, some channels had high cost per lead, and leadership lacked clear visibility into which campaigns were producing the best return.

Marketing needed a structured ROI optimization plan that could improve campaign tracking, reduce inefficient spend, increase qualified leads, and connect marketing activity to measurable business outcomes.

## Project Goals

- Improve campaign ROI through better performance tracking and budget allocation
- Increase lead quality and conversion rates
- Reduce cost per lead and wasted spend from underperforming channels
- Identify the highest-performing campaign channels
- Create a KPI dashboard for marketing performance visibility
- Improve communication between marketing, sales, leadership, and analytics stakeholders
- Build a repeatable campaign review process for future optimization

| Deliverable | Purpose |

| Risk Register | Documents campaign risks, impact, likelihood, owners, and mitigation plans |

| Source Data | Supports analysis of spend, leads, conversions, ROI, and channel performance |
| Meeting Notes | Shows campaign review decisions, blockers, recommendations, and follow-up actions |
| Implementation Playbook | Explains the campaign optimization process, reporting cadence, budget review, and escalation steps |
| Dashboard Readout | Interprets campaign KPIs and recommends next-step business actions |
| Resume & Interview Talking Points | Converts the project into job-ready language for resumes and interviews |

## Business Impact

This project demonstrates how a Marketing Manager can improve campaign performance by turning scattered marketing data into a structured ROI optimization system. The project connects campaign spend, channel performance, lead quality, conversion tracking, budget decisions, and leadership reporting into one organized improvement plan.

The optimized campaign process helps reduce wasted ad spend, increase qualified leads, improve conversion performance, strengthen sales and marketing alignment, and give leadership clearer visibility into marketing ROI.
