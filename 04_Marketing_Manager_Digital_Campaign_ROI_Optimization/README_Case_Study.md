# Marketing Manager: Digital Campaign ROI Optimization

## Target Role
Marketing Manager / Growth Marketing Manager

## Industry Context
Digital marketing / e-commerce

## Business Problem
Paid social and search spend increased, but lead conversion and return on ad spend were below target.

## Project Goal
Improve ROAS from 2.1x to 3.0x, lower cost per lead from $48 to $34, and increase qualified lead conversion from 7.8% to 11%.

## Management Approach
Reallocated budget to higher-intent campaigns, refreshed landing page messaging, added audience segmentation, and introduced weekly creative performance reviews.

## Results Summary
| KPI | Baseline | Target | Final Result | Status |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| ROAS | 2.1x | 3.0x | 3.3x | Exceeded |
| Cost Per Lead | $48 | $34 | $31 | Exceeded |
| Qualified Lead Conversion | 7.8% | 11.0% | 11.6% | Exceeded |
| Landing Page CVR | 3.2% | 4.5% | 4.8% | Exceeded |
| Budget Waste | 22% | 10% | 8% | Exceeded |

## Manager Skills Demonstrated
- KPI ownership and performance management
- Stakeholder communication and meeting facilitation
- Cross-functional planning and execution
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Process improvement and operational control
- Data-informed decision-making

## Final Business Decision
Scale high-intent paid search and retargeting while keeping weak audiences capped until conversion quality improves.
