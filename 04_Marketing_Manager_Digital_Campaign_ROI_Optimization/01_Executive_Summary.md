# Executive Summary

## Project
Marketing Manager: Digital Campaign ROI Optimization

## Executive Overview
The business was facing a measurable performance gap: Paid social and search spend increased, but lead conversion and return on ad spend were below target. The project was launched to create structure, accountability, and measurable improvement across the operating process.

## Objective
Improve ROAS from 2.1x to 3.0x, lower cost per lead from $48 to $34, and increase qualified lead conversion from 7.8% to 11%.

## Strategy
Reallocated budget to higher-intent campaigns, refreshed landing page messaging, added audience segmentation, and introduced weekly creative performance reviews.

## Outcome
The project delivered measurable improvement across the core KPIs. The strongest evidence of success was the final KPI dashboard, which shows improved performance versus baseline and target.

## Recommendation
Scale high-intent paid search and retargeting while keeping weak audiences capped until conversion quality improves.

## Why This Project Matters for Manager Roles
This project demonstrates the ability to diagnose a business problem, organize people around a plan, measure progress, manage risks, and communicate results in a way leaders can act on.
