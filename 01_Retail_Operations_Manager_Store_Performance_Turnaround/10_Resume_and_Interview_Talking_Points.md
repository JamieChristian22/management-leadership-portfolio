## Interview Answer: Tell Me About This Project
I led a management project focused on a clear business problem: Store labor cost, shrink, and customer wait times were trending above target while customer satisfaction was declining. My goal was to create a practical system that improved performance, gave leaders visibility, and helped the team execute consistently. I built the charter, KPI dashboard, risk register, stakeholder map, and action plan. The project improved the core metrics and produced a repeatable operating rhythm that could continue after the project ended.
