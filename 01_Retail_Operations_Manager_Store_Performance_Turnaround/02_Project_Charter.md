# Project Charter

## Project Name
Retail Operations Manager: Store Performance Turnaround

## Sponsor
Director of Operations

## Project Manager / Owner
Operations Manager

## Background
Store labor cost, shrink, and customer wait times were trending above target while customer satisfaction was declining.

## Business Need
The organization needed a practical management system that improved performance without adding unnecessary complexity for frontline teams or business partners.

## Scope
### In Scope
- KPI definition and dashboard creation
- Stakeholder alignment meetings
- Process redesign and operating cadence
- Risk register and mitigation ownership
- Training, communication, and adoption tracking
- Final business recommendation

### Out of Scope
- Enterprise-wide system replacement
- New headcount approval
- Long-term vendor contract renegotiation beyond the project window

## Success Criteria
- Labor Cost % improves from 18.9% to at least 16.8%.
- Avg Wait Time improves from 8.4 min to at least 5.5 min.
- NPS improves from 52 to at least 66.
- Shrink % improves from 3.8% to at least 2.6%.
- Schedule Adherence improves from 74% to at least 90%.

## Timeline
Start: 2026-01-06  
End: 2026-04-30

## Governance
- Weekly working session with functional leads
- Biweekly sponsor update
- End-of-project executive readout

## Final Approval Criteria
The project is considered successful when the KPI targets are met, risks are controlled, and the process can continue as a standard operating rhythm.
