# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Role in Project | Influence | Interest | Communication Plan |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Executive Sponsor | Approves priorities and removes blockers | High | High | Biweekly executive summary with KPI trend and risks |
| Department Lead | Owns local execution and team coaching | High | High | Weekly working session and action-item review |
| Frontline Team | Executes process changes and provides feedback | Medium | High | Daily huddles, quick reference guide, feedback channel |
| Analyst / Coordinator | Maintains data, dashboards, and issue logs | Medium | Medium | Twice-weekly data quality check-in |
| Finance Partner | Validates cost, savings, and business impact | Medium | Medium | Monthly KPI and savings validation |
| Customer / Employee User Group | Experiences the process outcome | Low | High | Survey and feedback review at milestone points |

## Stakeholder Management Strategy
The project used a high-touch communication plan for high-influence stakeholders and simple, repeatable updates for execution teams. This helped reduce resistance and kept decisions connected to measurable outcomes.
