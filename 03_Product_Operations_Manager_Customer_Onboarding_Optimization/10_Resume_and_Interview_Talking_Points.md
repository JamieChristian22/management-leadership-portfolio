## Interview Answer: Tell Me About This Project
I led a management project focused on a clear business problem: New customers were taking too long to activate, creating churn risk and additional support tickets. My goal was to create a practical system that improved performance, gave leaders visibility, and helped the team execute consistently. I built the charter, KPI dashboard, risk register, stakeholder map, and action plan. The project improved the core metrics and produced a repeatable operating rhythm that could continue after the project ended.
