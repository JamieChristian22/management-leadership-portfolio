# Product Operations Manager: Customer Onboarding Optimization

## Target Role
Product Operations Manager / Customer Success Manager

## Industry Context
SaaS / digital product

## Business Problem
New customers were taking too long to activate, creating churn risk and additional support tickets.

## Project Goal
Increase 30-day activation from 54% to 72%, reduce time-to-first-value from 12 days to 6 days, and cut onboarding-related tickets by 25%.

## Management Approach
Redesigned onboarding milestones, added lifecycle emails, introduced a customer health score, and created escalation criteria for at-risk accounts.

## Results Summary
| KPI | Baseline | Target | Final Result | Status |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 30-Day Activation | 54% | 72% | 76% | Exceeded |
| Time to First Value | 12 days | 6 days | 5.8 days | Exceeded |
| Onboarding Tickets | 420/month | 315/month | 302/month | Exceeded |
| Health Score Coverage | 20% | 95% | 96% | Met |
| Expansion-Ready Accounts | 31 | 45 | 48 | Exceeded |

## Manager Skills Demonstrated
- KPI ownership and performance management
- Stakeholder communication and meeting facilitation
- Cross-functional planning and execution
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Process improvement and operational control
- Data-informed decision-making

## Final Business Decision
Keep the milestone-based onboarding model and add health-score alerts to weekly customer success reviews.
