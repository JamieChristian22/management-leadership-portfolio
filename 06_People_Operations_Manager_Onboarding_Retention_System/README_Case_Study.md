# People Operations Manager: New-Hire Onboarding and Retention System

## Target Role
People Operations Manager / HR Operations Manager

## Industry Context
Corporate HR / people operations

## Business Problem
New hires were missing onboarding milestones, managers had inconsistent check-ins, and 90-day retention was below target.

## Project Goal
Increase 90-day retention from 81% to 91%, improve onboarding completion from 68% to 95%, and raise new-hire satisfaction from 3.6 to 4.4 out of 5.

## Management Approach
Built a 30-60-90 onboarding system with manager checklists, automated reminders, buddy assignments, and weekly completion reporting.

## Results Summary
| KPI | Baseline | Target | Final Result | Status |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 90-Day Retention | 81% | 91% | 92% | Exceeded |
| Onboarding Completion | 68% | 95% | 96% | Met |
| New-Hire Satisfaction | 3.6/5 | 4.4/5 | 4.5/5 | Exceeded |
| Manager Check-In Completion | 54% | 90% | 93% | Exceeded |
| Buddy Assignment Rate | 40% | 95% | 97% | Exceeded |

## Manager Skills Demonstrated
- KPI ownership and performance management
- Stakeholder communication and meeting facilitation
- Cross-functional planning and execution
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Process improvement and operational control
- Data-informed decision-making

## Final Business Decision
Adopt the 30-60-90 onboarding system as standard practice and review new-hire risk flags weekly.
