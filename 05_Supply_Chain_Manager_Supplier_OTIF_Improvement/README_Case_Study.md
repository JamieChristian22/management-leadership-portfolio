# Supply Chain Manager: Supplier OTIF Improvement

## Target Role
Supply Chain Manager / Procurement Operations Manager

## Industry Context
Manufacturing / distribution

## Business Problem
Late supplier deliveries caused production delays, expedited freight costs, and missed customer shipment commitments.

## Project Goal
Raise supplier OTIF from 82% to 94%, reduce expedited freight by 35%, and cut average supplier response time from 42 hours to 18 hours.

## Management Approach
Built supplier scorecards, created escalation SLAs, revised reorder points for critical materials, and launched monthly supplier business reviews.

## Results Summary
| KPI | Baseline | Target | Final Result | Status |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Supplier OTIF | 82% | 94% | 95% | Exceeded |
| Expedited Freight Cost | $64,000 | $41,600 | $38,500 | Exceeded |
| Supplier Response Time | 42 hrs | 18 hrs | 16 hrs | Exceeded |
| Production Delays | 18/month | 8/month | 6/month | Exceeded |
| Critical Stockout Events | 9/month | 3/month | 2/month | Exceeded |

## Manager Skills Demonstrated
- KPI ownership and performance management
- Stakeholder communication and meeting facilitation
- Cross-functional planning and execution
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Process improvement and operational control
- Data-informed decision-making

## Final Business Decision
Continue supplier scorecards and move chronic late suppliers to corrective action plans or alternate sourcing.
