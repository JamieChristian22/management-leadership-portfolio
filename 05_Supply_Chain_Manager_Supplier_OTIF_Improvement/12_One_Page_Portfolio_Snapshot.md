# One-Page Portfolio Snapshot

## Project
Supply Chain Manager Supplier OTIF Improvement

## Role Simulated
Supply Chain Manager / Supplier Performance Manager

## Business Problem
Supplier delivery issues were causing missed OTIF targets, production delays, stockouts, and rising expedited freight costs.

## Management Actions Taken
1. Diagnosed the baseline performance problem using KPI trends and operating data.
2. Built a stakeholder plan to align leaders, team members, and cross-functional partners.
3. Created a 30/60/90-day action plan with accountable owners, due dates, and success measures.
4. Managed risk through a working risk register and weekly escalation process.
5. Delivered measurable results and translated outcomes into resume-ready accomplishments.

## Final Results
- Supplier OTIF improved from 82% to 95% against a target of 94% (Exceeded).
- Expedited Freight Cost improved from $64,000 to $38,500 against a target of $41,600 (Exceeded).
- Supplier Response Time improved from 42 hrs to 16 hrs against a target of 18 hrs (Exceeded).
- Production Delays improved from 18/month to 6/month against a target of 8/month (Exceeded).
- Critical Stockout Events improved from 9/month to 2/month against a target of 3/month (Exceeded).

## Skills Demonstrated
- People leadership and stakeholder communication
- KPI ownership and performance management
- Process improvement and operating rhythm design
- Risk management and issue escalation
- Dashboard storytelling and executive reporting
- Change management and adoption planning
