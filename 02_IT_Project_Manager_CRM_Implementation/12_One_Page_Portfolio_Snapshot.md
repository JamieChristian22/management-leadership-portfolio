# One-Page Portfolio Snapshot

## Project
IT Project Manager CRM Implementation

## Role Simulated
IT Project Manager / Business Project Manager

## Business Problem
The sales team lacked consistent CRM adoption, clean pipeline data, and daily sales visibility for leadership reporting.

## Management Actions Taken
1. Diagnosed the baseline performance problem using KPI trends and operating data.
2. Built a stakeholder plan to align leaders, team members, and cross-functional partners.
3. Created a 30/60/90-day action plan with accountable owners, due dates, and success measures.
4. Managed risk through a working risk register and weekly escalation process.
5. Delivered measurable results and translated outcomes into resume-ready accomplishments.

## Final Results
- User Adoption improved from 0% to 93% against a target of 90% (Exceeded).
- Migrated Records improved from 0 to 4,812 against a target of 4,800 (Met).
- Duplicate Rate improved from 11.0% to 2.2% against a target of 3.0% (Exceeded).
- Pipeline Report Refresh improved from Weekly to Daily against a target of Daily (Met).
- UAT Pass Rate improved from 0% to 97% against a target of 95% (Exceeded).

## Skills Demonstrated
- People leadership and stakeholder communication
- KPI ownership and performance management
- Process improvement and operating rhythm design
- Risk management and issue escalation
- Dashboard storytelling and executive reporting
- Change management and adoption planning

## How to Present This in an Interview
“I built this project as a realistic manager case study. I started with a business problem, defined measurable KPIs, created the plan, managed stakeholders and risks, then documented the final results. The project shows that I can think like a manager, not just complete tasks.”
